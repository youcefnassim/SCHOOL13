How to Apply

A simple, step-by-step guide to our admissions process

Step 1: Submit Application

Complete the online application form or download the PDF version. Ensure all required information is provided accurately.

Step 2: Document Submission

Submit all required documents, including academic records, birth certificate, and photographs, to the admissions office.

Step 3: Entrance Assessment

Prospective students will be scheduled for an entrance assessment to evaluate their academic readiness and skills.

Step 4: Family Interview

A meeting with the parents/guardians and the student to discuss our educational philosophy and answer any questions.

Step 5: Admission Decision

The admissions committee will review the application, and a decision will be communicated to the family within two weeks.
